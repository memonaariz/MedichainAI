❓ Help & Support

Frequently asked questions and how-to guides

🚀 Quick Start

How do I get a prescription?

Ask your doctor to log in to MediChain and write a digital prescription for you. It will appear automatically in your Prescriptions section.

How do reminders work?

When a doctor writes a prescription, reminders are created automatically. You can also add personal reminders via Snap Prescription. Allow notifications for timely alerts.

What is the QR code on my profile?

Your unique patient QR code. Show it to your doctor — they can scan it to instantly view your medical history and write prescriptions.

How does pharmacy dispensing work?

Your prescription has an encrypted QR code. Show it to a registered MediChain pharmacy. They scan it, decrypt it with their pharmacy key, and dispense your medicines. Each prescription can only be dispensed once.

How do I upload a lab report?

Go to Lab Reports → Upload PDF. The system automatically extracts values and compares them with normal ranges. Use text-based PDFs from labs like Dr. Lal PathLabs, SRL, Thyrocare.

How do I use Emergency SOS?

Go to Emergency SOS and press the big red button. Your GPS location will be sent to all your emergency contacts and family members immediately.

🔗 About MediChain

MediChain is a blockchain-secured healthcare management platform. All prescriptions are hashed with SHA-256 and stored on an immutable blockchain. Pharmacy dispensing uses AES-256 encryption. Your medical data is tamper-proof and permanently verifiable.
